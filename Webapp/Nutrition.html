OUR NUTRITION

Diet List


Protein Sources:

i. Lean meats (e.g., chicken, turkey, lean beef)

ii. Fish (e.g., salmon, tuna, trout)

iii. Eggs

iv. Dairy products (e.g., Greek yogurt, cottage cheese)


Carbohydrate Sources:

i. Whole grains (e.g., brown rice, quinoa, whole wheat pasta)

ii. Whole-grain bread and cereals

iii. Vegetables (e.g., broccoli, spinach, carrots)


Fiber-Rich Foods:

i. Whole grains

ii. Nuts and seeds

iii. Legumes

iv. Bran cereals


Limit Added Sugars:

i. Minimize the intake of sugary snacks, sodas, and sugary cereals.

i. Choose naturally sweet foods like fruits for a healthier option.


Portion Control:

i. Pay attention to portion sizes to avoid overeating.


Meal Balance:

i. Aim for balanced meals that include a protein source, a source of carbohydrates, and plenty of vegetables or fruits.


Whole Food Snacks:

i. Opt for whole food snacks like fresh fruit, yogurt, nuts, or vegetable sticks with hummus.
